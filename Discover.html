Ferarri World

Whether you crave the intense G-force of world-class rollercoasters, want to go-kart race with your family or challenge the best lap times in a state-of-the-art simulator – Ferarri World has endless fun for everyone. This out of the world theme park features the world’s fastest roller coaster, known as the Formula Rossa with a top speed of 155mph in 4.9 seconds. family-friendly attractions, state-of-the-art simulators, electric-powered go-karts, live shows and an inspiring collection of racing memorabilia, in the souvenir store.

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo

Do you want to explore this out of the world aquarium? While at Dubai do not miss the 10,000,0000 litre tank measuring at 51 metres in length, 20 metres in width and 11 metres in height, making it one of the largest aquariums in the world. This wonderous attraction contains over 33,000 aquatic animals and it is home to over 140 sepcies of sea life. Furthermore, you can go snorkelling inside the tank so that you can view the different species of sea life at close range, which is a once in a life time oppurtunity. If you are short on time you can take in all the highlights in an hour with the all-access private guided tour, which includes a glass bottom boat ride and fish feeding experience.

The Dubai Fountain

If you are visitng Dubai you must visit the Fountain, which is a must-see destination. To ensure you do not miss the oppurtunity to view the amazing fountain show, there are 13 shows daily (at 30 minute intervals) and the timings are as follows; 1pm to 1.30pm and 6pm to 11pm daily (and 1.30pm to 2pm on Fridays). The Dubai Fountain offers a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to immerse yourself in a captivating water, music and light experience.Stretching over 275 metres in length, the Dubai Fountain is the world’s tallest performing fountain.So if you are planning a visit to the Arabian Haven, you must visit the Dubai Fountain which is located at the foot of the Dubai Mall.
